Plan - Garden of Peace Memorial Park

Site name:

The name of the site is Garden of Peace
a posible URL would be: www.restingop.com
I chose this name because the place I work has this same name but in spanish, it represents a garden where we can have our loved ones that are resting in peace.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the site is to emulate a real memorial park and funeral home company, that will allow users to know more about this company and buy certificates and places in Garden of Peace.

Scenarios:

I am looking for a place in Garden of Peace, What shoud I do?
You should go to the page, then you have two options, you can navigate to the bottom of the home page, to the Contact Us section and click on "Buy a Certificate", or you can directly go to the contact us page in the navigation bar.
What can I do to know more about plans and certificates?
You can look in the Plans section, or directly go to the Plans page through the navigation bar, you will find there more information about certificates and places, the different plans and how is the process to buy one.
Someone passed away, Help!
There's no time to waste, click on the "I have an Emergency" button, there are two located at the top and the bottom of the page. It will lead you directly to a form where you will complete the information about the deceased person and the responsible person for the service.
